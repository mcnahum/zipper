# Privacy Policy

**Zipper for macOS**
Last updated: April 2026

## Overview

Zipper does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information of any kind.

## What We Collect

Nothing. Zipper operates entirely on your device. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking, no identifiers.

## Third Parties

Zipper uses the 7zz binary (7-Zip) under the hood to handle compression and extraction. This binary runs locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any data. No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms are used.

## Network Access

Zipper does not make any network requests. All operations — compression, extraction, and encryption — happen locally on your Mac.

## Your Files

Zipper processes your files locally. Your files never leave your device and are never accessed by us or anyone else.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date at the top.
